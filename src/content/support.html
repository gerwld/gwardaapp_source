Having troubles with GwardaApp?

Here are some troubleshooting steps you can try:

  • Refresh the Amazon page.
  • Disable and then enable again GwardaApp.
  • Disable any other extensions.
  • Uninstall and re-install GwardaApp.
  • Clear browser cache.

If the problem persist, please email at pjaworski.dev@gmail.com.

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include this information:

  • The version of GwardaApp you are using.
  • A screenshot or video demonstrating the problem.
  • A detailed description of the issue.
  • Your browser and its version (e.g., Firefox 86.0).
